01 — About

What I do
and why

I'm a Full Stack Engineer with 7 years of experience building mobile and web products. I work with startups, non-technical founders, and engineering teams: early-stage ideas, existing systems that need improving, and everything in between.

The work varies a lot. Sometimes that means sitting with a founder who has a rough idea and no technical background, and helping shape it into something real. Other times it's jumping into a codebase and making it faster, more maintainable, or ready to scale. I've built products from scratch across more than ten domains, and worked with teams in Canada, the Netherlands, and the UK.

"What I care about is whether what gets built is something people actually find useful. That's the part that sticks."

Research Tool · 2025

PDF Intelligence POC

A research team was pulling structured data out of PDFs by hand. It was slow and easy to get wrong, so the two of us built a proof of concept that showed the whole process could be automated. We tested several AI models, kept the two that performed best, and paired them with a separate computer vision service for the harder document layouts. It all fed into a React app where the team could review and use what came out.

PythonReact Vertex AIAI SDK PDF Parsing

Golazo AI

A football training app for young players. They record their drills, get scored on technique, and can see how they improve over time. I built the mobile side, and it's live on the App Store and Google Play with thousands of real users. When the design called for a text style React Native doesn't support, I built the component myself and published it as an open-source package on npm.

React NativeiOS Androidnpm
golazo.ai npm: outlined-text

BYOB Sri Lanka

A directory of BYOB restaurants across Sri Lanka. People use it to find places that let them bring their own drinks and to check corkage rules before they go. My brother started it and I came in later. I moved the whole thing off Replit onto a proper Next.js setup, tightened up the security, and rebuilt the events and admin features. I still keep it running today, and it has a steady base of regulars who keep coming back.
