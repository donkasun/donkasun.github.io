What I do
and why

I'm a Full Stack Engineer with 7 years of experience building mobile and web products. I work with startups, non-technical founders, and engineering teams: early-stage ideas, existing systems that need improving, and everything in between.

The work varies a lot. Sometimes that means sitting with a founder who has a rough idea and no technical background, and helping shape it into something real. Other times it's jumping into a codebase and making it faster, more maintainable, or ready to scale. I've built products from scratch across more than ten domains, and worked with teams in Canada, the Netherlands, and the UK.

"What I care about is whether what gets built is something people actually find useful. That's the part that sticks."
